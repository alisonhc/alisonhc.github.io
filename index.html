Alison Chi

I am a first-year PhD student at the University of Edinburgh's ILCC supervised by Emily Allaway. My current research focuses on how we express and interpret implied meaning in language, and to what extent language models can reason about this.

Google Scholar  /  LinkedIn  

Background
I received a Master's in computer science from National Tsing Hua University in Taiwan, where I worked on controlled paraphrasing and was supervised by Jason S. Chang. For my undergraduate, I studied both linguistics and computer science at Macalester College and was supervised by Shilad Sen.
